About Us

We are a fitness company that focuses on your fitness goals! At Mor Athletic, we offer fitness services in the city of Chicago that range from Personal Training, Group Fitness, Sports Training and Yoga at the convenience of our clients.

Reaching your goal is our commitment and mission. To make this possible for our clients, we offer training at our clients' homes, at partnership gyms, and even outdoors!

Allow us to help you reach your goals. We want you to push your limits. We want to give you confidence in your strength. We want you to become MOR ATHLETIC!
